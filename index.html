FAQ

Understandably, we have been asked several questions. Here are some of the frequent questions people have asked. If you have a question that does not appear below, please don’t hesitate to text/call/email Gabby or Brett.

What/where is this place Logan Square Auditorium?

Although it’s a great spot to catch a movie and grab a drink, this is NOT the more commonly known Logan Theater. Logan Square Auditorium is a historic ballroom that was built in 1915. It now serves as a banquet hall and concert venue and is located on the third floor of a multi-purpose building. The address is 2539 N. Kedzie Blvd. and the entrance is through a glass door next to Lula Cafe.

How do I get there?

Logan Auditorium is conveniently located two blocks from the Logan Square stop of the Blue Line. Otherwise Uber is always a great option. If you have to drive, there will be a parking lot available for a limited amount of cars.

If I’m driving, where should I park? Will there be valet?

There is no valet. If you are driving, there is a parking lot behind the building that can fit 30 cars. You will have to walk around the building, about a block, to get to the entrance.

What is the attire? What will Gabby and Brett be wearing?

Please wear business casual attire. Gabby and Brett will be dressed up- this is a celebration of our marriage after all!

It’s Halloween weekend! Can I wear a costume?

For those of you who care enough that we are taking up your Halloween weekend, 1) Thank you for sacrificing the evening to celebrate with us, and 2) Yes, if it’s that important to you, you can certainly wear a costume.

Are we going out after?

Of course! Gabby and Brett will have to clean up a bit and help close up, but we plan to go out after. There are several bars in the area. Location of meeting up after is TBD.

Can I bring my kid(s)?

That's up to your discrestion. We do not discourage children but please keep in mind this is an adult-centric party in that there will be drinking and a large amount of people.

Will there be dancing?

There better be! We will not be having a DJ, but will instead have a playlist of our favorite songs ranging the decades and genres. We’ve been to weddings where barely anyone dances and that;s no fun so please do your part in making our dance floor the place to be!

Where did you register?

We did not register anywhere. We are leaving gift-giving to your discretion.

Did you know your stupid wedding party falls on game 4 of the World Series?

Yes, we are aware, and yes we know (to Brett’s chagrin) that the Cubs might be playing and win the World Series that evening. Should this happen, we will do our best to have a way for everyone to watch. Go Cubbies!
